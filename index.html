Venue & Parking

Location: Greenwich Country Day School, Greenwich, CT

  • Main Entrance: Follow event signage to registration desks.
  • Parking: Free on-site parking for cars and vans. Bus parking available—please notify us in advance.
  • Drop-off: Use the main loop; volunteers will direct delegates to check-in.
  • Accessibility: ADA-compliant entrances and restrooms are available. Contact us to arrange any specific accommodations.
  • Map: Open in Google Maps

FAQ

What is the dress code?

Western business attire. National and cultural dress is welcome.

Do we submit position papers?

Yes—one page per topic. Email PDFs or DOCX to SBarhydt26@GCDS.net by March 14, 2026 at 11:59 PM ET.

Are meals provided?

Lunch is included with delegate fees. Vegetarian and gluten-free options available.

How are awards determined?

Chairs evaluate diplomacy, policy knowledge, collaboration, and writing. Awards include Best Delegate, Outstanding, and Honorable Mention per committee, plus Best Delegation.

Is technology allowed in committee?

Laptops and tablets are permitted at the chairs’ discretion for drafting. Messaging with other rooms or outside parties is not allowed.

Accessibility & accommodations?

We can arrange room placement, seating, or other accommodations. Email SBarhydt26@GCDS.net.
